In plain English: Kalma collects only what's needed to run the app — your email, name, and usage data like streaks and goal progress. We don't sell your data. Ever.

01 Information We Collect

When you create an account or use Kalma, we may collect the following:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Usage data — daily session completions, streaks, and goal journey progress
  • App preferences — session duration, reminder settings, and theme (morning/night)
  • Survey responses — optional answers provided during goal setup and completion
  • Device information — basic technical data (OS version, device type) used for crash reporting and compatibility
  • Crash and diagnostic data — anonymous crash reports collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs and improve stability

If you use Kalma as a guest, no personal information is collected. Data is stored locally on your device only.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalise your Kalma experience
  • Sync your progress and streak across devices
  • Send optional reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app stability via Crashlytics
  • Improve app features and fix issues
  • Respond to support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles.

03 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which provides industry-standard encryption both in transit and at rest.

Crash reports and diagnostic data are collected by Firebase Crashlytics, a Google service. Crashlytics data is anonymised and does not include your name or email address.

Local data (for guest users or offline use) is stored on your device using SharedPreferences and is not transmitted to any server.

Data location: Firebase services are operated by Google LLC and data may be stored on servers located in the United States or other countries where Google operates infrastructure.

04 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share data only with:

  • Google Firebase — for authentication, database, and crash reporting
  • Google Play — for processing subscription purchases and verifying entitlements
  • Law enforcement — only if required by applicable law

No other third parties receive your personal data.

05 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update your name or email in the app settings
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a readable format
  • Opt out of crash reporting — contact us to disable Crashlytics data collection for your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

Signing out of the app clears all locally cached personal data from your device immediately.

06 Children's Privacy

Kalma does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

07 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of Kalma after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
