Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Kalma account and all associated data at any time — directly in the app or by contacting us.

⚠ This cannot be undone. Deleting your account removes all your data immediately and permanently. There is no grace period and no way to recover your account once deleted.

How to delete your account in the app

1

Open Kalma and sign in

Make sure you are signed into the account you wish to delete.

2

Go to your Account

Tap the profile icon in the top right corner of the home screen.

3

Open Settings

Tap the ⚙ Settings icon in the account screen.

4

Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.

5

Confirm in the popup

A confirmation dialog lists everything that will be deleted. Tap "Yes, delete everything" to confirm. Your account and all data are deleted immediately.
